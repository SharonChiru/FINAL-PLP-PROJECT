ServiceConnect Hub

Established in 2024, ServiceConnect Hub is an innovative solution that provides a centralized platform that connects event service providers/vendors with clients, with the goal of streamlining the event planning process. Our platform acts as a centralised hub for effective communication, trust building and creates a vibrant environment that allows clients and event service providers to work together seamlessly.
